Laundry Service

Our PG offers a convenient and efficient laundry service to keep your clothes clean and fresh. Our laundry facilities are equipped with modern washing machines and dryers, ensuring your laundry is done quickly and effectively.

Key features of our laundry service include:

  • High-quality washing machines and dryers
  • Professional staff to assist you
  • Quick turnaround time
  • Affordable rates

To use our laundry service, simply drop off your clothes at the laundry room, and our staff will take care of the rest. You can also book the service online through our website.

How It Works

  1. Bring your dirty laundry to our designated laundry room.
  2. Fill out a service request form indicating your preferences (e.g., detergent type, washing temperature).
  3. Our staff will collect your laundry, process it according to your specifications, and notify you once it's ready for pickup.

Booking Options

Our laundry service can be booked through the following options:

  • Online Booking: Visit our website to fill out the online booking form.
  • On-site Booking: Book directly at the PG reception upon arrival.

Service Hours

Our laundry facilities are open from Monday to Saturday, from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

Available Time Slots (10 AM to 6 PM)







Pricing

Our laundry service prices are as follows:

  • Washing (per load): ₹50
  • Drying (per load): ₹40
  • Washing + Drying (per load): ₹80
  • Detergent (optional, per load): ₹10

Additional Information

  • Special Requests: We accommodate special requests such as delicate garment handling or specific washing instructions.
  • Payment: Payment for laundry services can be made at the reception desk.
  • Service Availability: Please note that service availability may vary during weekends and holidays.

Benefits

  • Convenience: Save time and effort with our on-site laundry facilities.
  • Quality Assurance: Rest assured that your clothes are in good hands with our professional laundry service.
  • Community Experience: Use our laundry room as an opportunity to interact with fellow residents and build a sense of community.
